RePurpose

This was a project started at ProtoHack in March 2015. Since then it has grown into a startup that's on its way to revolutionizing the way schools aquire goods. We aim to connect businesses with excess goods to schools in need. I met the team at ProtoHack and these are based off of the original wireframes I made in march. Darren, who originally had the idea, envisioned a website that functioned similarly to Craigslist, so I made a site to reflect that. Since then, we've moved in a different direction and we are going into our first phase of testing with the users.

You can learn more about RePurpose here.

Team Members:

Brandon Goldsworthy, Darren Varnado, Sarah Armstrong, Karen Rankin, and Laura Zuchlewski.

My Contributions:

  • Created wireframes for initial website layout and interaction.
  • Designed surveys for teachers, businesses, and others for online distribution to better understand the current state of goods disposal and acquisition.
  • Did a competitive analysis by shopping on competing websites to gain a better understanding of the strengths and weaknesses of potential competitors.
