Repurpose

This was a project started at ProtoHack in March 2015. Since then it has grown into a startup that's on its way to revolutionizing the way schools acquire goods. We aim to connect businesses with excess goods to schools in need. I met the team at ProtoHack and this mockup is based off of the original wireframes I made in March. Darren, who originally had the idea, envisioned a website that functioned similarly to Craigslist, so I made a site to reflect that. Since then, we've moved in a different direction and we are going into our first phase of testing with the users.

You can learn more about RePurpose here.

Team Members:

Darren Varnado, Brandon Goldsworthy, Sarah Armstrong, Karen Rankin, and Laura Zuchlewski.

My Contributions:

  • Created wireframes for initial website layout and interaction.
  • Designed surveys for teachers, businesses, and others for online distribution to better understand the current state of goods disposal and acquisition.
  • Conducted many phone interviews, using questions I'd written, to gain clear picture of the restraints on and desires of the users.
  • Created personas to better connect the group with potential users, using the research conducted through online surveys and phone interviews.
  • Did competitive analyses by shopping on competing websites to gain a better understanding of the strengths and weaknesses of potential competitors.
