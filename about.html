Molly Fallen

My name is Maureen, but I go by Molly. I am an undergraduate student at the University of Washington. I'm majoring in Human Centered Design and Engineering and specializing in Human Computer Interaction. I want to specialize in interaction design. I love solving problems by taking on different perspectives to anticipate people's needs.

My LinkedIn page can be found here.

In my spare time, I love to go to conventions. I go to PAX, a videogame convention, and SakuraCon, an anime convention, every year. They're a great way to meet people and have fun. PAX is a really unique experience. It's fun to meet with game developers, attend panels, and keep up to date on video game trends. Last PAX, I got a chance to play Lucky's Tale, a virtual reality platformer. It was very fun, and it got me interested in VR. That experience was what prompted me to go to the Seattle Mini VR Hackathon, to see if I could create a similarly wonderful experience for someone else.

I also like to do crafts. I sew, papercraft, and knit. I especially like crafting video game characters. I'm excited to continue creating and learning.
