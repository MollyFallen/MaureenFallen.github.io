Hackster's "Hack to the Future" Hardware Weekend - Portland, OR

Our team won Best Hack by creating a "flux capacitor" that can detect lightning strikes in the area, up to 40 km away. This could eventually be used by people in high risk areas to know when to seek shelter.

Team Members:

Maureen Fallen, Alex Trimble, Eric Thomas, Brian Bloom

My Contributions:

  • I used Fusion 360 to design and 3D print a smaller and more compact case for the prototype that could eventually be used in the workplace.
  • I decided on the visual design for the actual detector. We used the flux capacitor model already created by Eric and I painted it to resemble the flux capacitor in the movie.
  • I made design decisions for the app that could be paired with the device for a faster alert time.
