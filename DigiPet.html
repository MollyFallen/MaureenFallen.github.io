Digital Pet for the Moto 360

This is a personal project based on the idea of creating a digital pet designed for smartwatches. Smartwatches are often passive and I thought it would be fun to create something active. The pet can be fed and played with. Playing with the pet restores hearts and gives credits to buy more food or games.
