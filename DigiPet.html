Digital Pet for a Smartwatch

This is a personal project based on the idea of creating a digital pet designed for smartwatches. Smartwatches are often passive and it was fun to create something active. The pet will be able to be fed and played with. Playing with the pet restores hearts and gives credits to buy more food or games.
