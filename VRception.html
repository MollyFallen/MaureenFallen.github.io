VRception

We created a small puzzle game in 12 hours for the Oculus Rift and Leap Motion.

The idea behind this game was to use size and scale as a puzzle element by creating the world you are in, within the world you are in.

My Contributions:

  • Created the visual design for the game. I wanted it to look child-like and fun.
  • Created puzzle ideas for the demonstration.
